# Walkthrough

Guide through a series of tasks step by step.

## Process
1. Identify all steps needed to reach the goal
2. Present ONE step at a time
3. Wait for confirmation before proceeding
4. Provide clear instructions for each step
5. Track progress and remaining steps
6. Continue until the goal is complete

## Format
"Step X of Y: [Task description]"
[Detailed instructions]
"Ready for the next step?"
